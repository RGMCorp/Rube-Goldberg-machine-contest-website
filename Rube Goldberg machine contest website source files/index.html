Why this contest?

I don't really know what to put here

We felt that there was a lack of science based events in Halton. Students interested in STEM related subjects have been confined to attending events outside of Halton making it both inconvenient and discouraging for many students. We hope to change this by starting our own competition where high school students can express their creativity and scientific knowledge.

We invite STEM passionate students to come and attend our competition in hopes to create a Halton wide competition for our community.
